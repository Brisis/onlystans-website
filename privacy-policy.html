Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 24, 2025

1. Introduction

OnlyStans (Pty) Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of South Africa and international data protection standards.

2. Blockchain Transparency Disclaimer

Please Note: Your Solana wallet address and transaction history are public information stored permanently on the blockchain. OnlyStans cannot edit, delete, or hide information written to the blockchain.

While we protect your off-chain data (such as your email or profile settings), we have no control over the public ledger. You acknowledge that payments made via USDC are visible to the public.

3. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Username, email address (optional), and profile biography.
  • Financial Information: Public Solana wallet addresses. We do not collect or store your Private Keys or Seed Phrases.
  • Usage Data: Device information, IP address, and interaction logs to improve platform performance and security.
  • Content Data: Images, videos, and text uploaded to the platform.

4. How We Use Your Information

We process your data to:

  • Facilitate crypto payments and subscriptions.
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations under South African law.
  • Send service notifications regarding your account.

5. Data Storage and Security

We employ industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to protect data in transit and at rest. Your off-chain data is stored on secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your wallet credentials confidential.

6. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your off-chain personal data.
  • Object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

7. Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact our Information Officer via Telegram: @onlystanschannel
